Meet Bella! A local artist from Ottawa interested in fiber arts, visual media and
creative experimentation.



Bella's inspirations include ceramic artists, such as Florian Gadsby , as well as physical media painters
like Christi Belcourt.

She enjoys using bright colors and interesting textures to experiment within her works. Nostalgia
and childlike perspectives play a large role in her works.

Please reach out to Bella on any of her social media for any questions or requests you may have.
You can find her on Instagram or TikTok, or in the Algonquin College library.
